Another Fan Made Rogue One Expanded Teaser Trailer

YouTube user stryder HD has gone above and beyond in his Rogue One teaser trailer. Here included are clips not only from the Star Wars saga episodes I-VI but also from other performances from Felicity Jones. They don't quite work, but it's interesting to give a meta-context to the new film... casting known actors as leads is kind of a new thing. Fun to watch regardless.
