What Rey Really Heard

FINALLY! Our copies of The Force Awakens have arrived and we're able to analyze the film frame by frame, sound by sound. We're throwing as many tools as we can get our hands on to find out every secret, easter egg, and in-joke we can find. Youtube User Ross Smith has made an important discovery by isolating the exact sounds that Rey hears as she's beaconed further into Maz's dungeon.
